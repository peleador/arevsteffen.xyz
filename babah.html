Babah

Web Design

Babah is an online babysitter service which was born out of necessity for parents to hire qualified professionals who can take care of their children whenever needed. An innovative project that aims to solve a problem which is quite common for middle class parents who wish free time from their kids to go out and socialize. Babysitting is not a strong culture in Brazil, it was launched in 2017 so it is still in development. Nevertheless the first contact with the public target was a success so the product model is promising.
